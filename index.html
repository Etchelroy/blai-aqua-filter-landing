The Problem

Your Tap Water Is Hiding
a Dangerous Secret

The EPA has identified over 90 regulated contaminants in U.S. water supplies — and thousands more go unchecked every day.

PFAS "Forever Chemicals"

Found in 45% of U.S. tap water. Linked to cancer, thyroid disease, and immune disorders. EPA now classifies these as hazardous at any detectable level.

Lead & Heavy Metals

6 million lead service lines still active in the U.S. Even trace amounts cause neurological damage, especially in children under 6 years old.

Microplastics

Detected in 93% of bottled water and now found in human blood. Long-term health impact is still being studied — early results are deeply alarming.

Chlorine & DBPs

Disinfection byproducts form when chlorine reacts with organic matter. Associated with bladder cancer and adverse reproductive outcomes in long-term exposure studies.
