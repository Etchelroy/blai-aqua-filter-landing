How Our 5-Stage Filtration Works

Each stage targets specific contaminant classes for comprehensive protection.

1

Sediment Pre-Filter

2

Activated Carbon Block

Eliminates chlorine, chloramine, VOCs, herbicides, and taste/odour compounds with a 0.5-micron carbon block.

3

Reverse Osmosis Membrane

The core stage. Forces water through a 0.0001-micron semi-permeable membrane, rejecting lead, arsenic, nitrates, PFAS, fluoride, and 70+ more.

4

DI Polishing Stage

Deionisation resin captures any remaining dissolved solids, delivering lab-pure water with TDS < 5 ppm.

5

Remineralisation + UV

Adds back calcium and magnesium for great taste, then UV-C light delivers a final kill-step against any residual bacteria or viruses.
